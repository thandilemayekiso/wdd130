The history of whitewater rafting dates back to the 16th century. It's a unique and exciting adventure that combines the thrill of riding a boat with the beautiful waters of South Africa.

history

Whitewater rafting has its roots in the 16th century when English settlers sailed on the Grand Banks of the North Sea. The first whitewater rafting trip took place in 1630. Over the next few decades, the adventure grew in popularity and became an important part of the English folklore and culture.

Today, whitewater rafting is a popular activity that brings people together and offers a unique and exciting adventure. It's a great way to connect with friends, family, and even the local community. With so many amazing whitewater rafting trips happening around the world, it's essential to enjoy this amazing experience.
